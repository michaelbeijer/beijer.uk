[SuperLookup](https://superlookup.io/) is a free desktop app that lets you research a term across all your terminology sources at once – Beijerterm, IATE, Linguee, ProZ, Glosbe, termbases, corpora, Wikipedia and 40+ more – each in its own embedded, ad-free tab. Select a term in any application, press a single keystroke, and SuperLookup opens at the front with everything already searched, your logins remembered and trackers blocked – all in one window instead of scattered across your browser. No install, no account; Windows, macOS and Linux. It's the standalone version of the SuperLookup feature in Supervertaler, and also comes built into the Supervertaler Workbench alongside your own termbases and translation memories.
