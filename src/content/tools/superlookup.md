[SuperLookup](https://superlookup.io/) is a free desktop app that opens every terminology and reference site a translator reaches for – Superterm, IATE, Linguee, ProZ, Glosbe, Wikipedia and 35+ more – each in its own embedded, ad-free tab. Select a term in any application, press a global hotkey, and SuperLookup jumps to the front already searching it, with your logins remembered and trackers blocked. No install, no account; Windows, macOS and Linux. It's the standalone version of the SuperLookup feature in Supervertaler, and also comes built into the Supervertaler Workbench alongside your own termbases and translation memories.
