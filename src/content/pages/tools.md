<p>I build and maintain tools that support real-world translation workflows.</p>
<h2>Supervertaler&nbsp;</h2><p><a href="https://supervertaler.com">Supervertaler</a>&nbsp;is an open-source,&nbsp;cross-platform AI-enhanced CAT tool. </p><p>It features two special tools that can be&nbsp;run inside Supervertaler&nbsp;or system-wide (on Mac/Linux/Windows):</p><ol><li>‘Supervertaler QuickMenu’ –&nbsp;a right-click launcher menu: run any saved AI prompt against the current segment directly from the context menu, with results shown or inserted in-place.</li><li>‘Supervertaler QuickTrans’ –&nbsp;an instant multi-engine translation popup: fetches MT and LLM translations in parallel and lets you pick one by pressing its number. Similar to <a href="https://gt4t.ai/default/" target="_blank">GT4T</a>.</li></ol><h2>Beijerterm.com</h2><p><a href="https://beijerterm.com/" target="_blank">Beijerterm</a>&nbsp;is an open multilingual terminology database.&nbsp;Beijerterm.com is possibly the world's largest collection of specialist Dutch/English terminology, built up over 30 years of professional translation work. I hope it will be of particular interest to fellow translators and terminologists.</p>
