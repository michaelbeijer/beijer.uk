<p>I build and maintain tools that support real-world translation workflows and terminology management.</p>
<h2>Supervertaler&nbsp;</h2>
<p><a href="https://supervertaler.com">Supervertaler</a>&nbsp;is a cross-platform, AI-enhanced CAT tool. It includes two tools that also run system-wide on Windows, Mac and Linux:</p>
<ol><li><strong>QuickMenu</strong> –&nbsp;a right-click prompt launcher: run any saved AI prompt against the current segment from the context menu, with results shown or inserted in-place.</li><li><strong>QuickTrans</strong> –&nbsp;an instant multi-engine translation popup (think <a href="https://gt4t.ai/default/" target="_blank">GT4T</a>): fetches MT and LLM translations in parallel and lets you pick one by pressing its number.</li></ol>
<h2>Beijerterm.com</h2>
<p><a href="https://beijerterm.com/" target="_blank">Beijerterm</a>&nbsp;is an open multilingual terminology database – one of the largest collections of specialist Dutch/English terminology available, built up over 30 years of professional translation work. Aimed at fellow translators and terminologists.</p>
