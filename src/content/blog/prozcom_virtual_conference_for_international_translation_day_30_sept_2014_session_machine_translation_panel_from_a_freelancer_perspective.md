I was a panellist in the ProZ.com virtual conference for International Translation Day (30 Sept 2014), in the session 'Machine translation panel from a freelancer perspective'. The session was moderated by Jeff Allen and included Dimitra Anastasiou, Patrick Porter, and myself as panellists, with around 105 people watching live.

The discussion was structured as a roundtable covering four main topics: each panellist's MT workflow, how to handle confidentiality when using online MT engines, the impact of post-editing, and which language pairs and directions lend themselves best to MT.
